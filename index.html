ALIEN JUNGLE
BRANDING | POSTER & COSTUME DESIGN | ILLUSTRATOR
I had the opportunity to work with the New World Symphony in Miami, FL to put on a percussion concort. The theme was Alien Jungle and the goal was to submerse the audience in a "new world". As a designer, I created the branding for the concert. My team and I used my branding concepts as a guideline for deciding on lighting and projections, promtional posters, and orchestral costumes. CAR SEAT
CAR SEAT
3D MODELING & CONCEPT DESIGN | RENDERING & LIGHTING | MAYA
I designed, modeled, and rendered this car seat using Autodesk Maya. Its function and design allows for the child to have their own shade for warmer months. It also has air condition built into the seat and is easily removed from the car to be used as a carrier when out and about. It is built on an axis so that it can offset the motion of a car coming to a quick stop, leaving the child virtually undisturbed.
